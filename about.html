Why I Chose Animal Crossing



I first discovered Animal Crossing during the COVID-19 lockdown in 2020.

It became everything for me and my boyfriend during those boring lockdown days. The game is very relaxing and doesn't make me too stressed. I love the music, visuals, and everything about it. I adore the residents on my island, and I feel like I have become friends with them. If I don't play the game for a few days, I miss them a lot.

So, even though I am busy with my studies now, I visit my old friends on the island every few days. I love this adorable game and everything about it!
